Lemon Poppyseed Muffins (vegan)

Recipe and video created by Ryoya Takashima. Want more yummy videos, go to Peaceful Cuisine.

See Ingredients

  • 300g all purpose flour
  • 230g apple sauce
  • 300g coconut milk
  • zest and juice of 2 lemons
  • 2 tbsp poppyseed
  • 1 tbsp baking powder

See Instructions

  1. combine all the ingredients together in a bowl.
  2. mix the mixture and pour it into a baking tin.
  3. bake at 350F/180C for 30 min
